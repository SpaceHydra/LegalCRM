Legal CRM Portal

A quick index of every HTML module and supporting resource so you can jump straight in.

Core Modules

  • Dashboard – seen-first welcome with live stats, quick actions, and activity logs.
  • Lead Management – full lead CRUD, scoring, aging, and conversion.
  • Client Management – master client records with SPOCs, PAN/GSTIN, and segmentation.
  • Project Management – lifecycle tracking, milestones, and timelines.
  • Task Management – priority handling, dependencies, and time logs.
  • Document Management – versions, tags, and access controls.
  • User & Role Management – RBAC, departments, and granular status.
  • Reporting – revenue trends, team metrics, and exportable analytics.
  • Billing – GST invoices, payments, and due alerts.
  • Calendar – meetings, agendas, and attendee tracking.
  • Static Dashboard – legacy snapshot of the original dashboard design.

Documentation & Guides

  • Functional Specification (FSD) – narrative of requirements, workflows, and module scope.
  • Usage Guide – walk-through for navigating and operating the prototype.
  • Sync Guide – explains how the supplied `sync.bat` and `sync.sh` keep your repo in sync.
  • Credentials Notes – placeholder for storing secure reference coordinates (keep it empty!).

Local Environment

All modules share the js/data.js data layer and css/common-styles.css styling. Launch a quick server to browse:

python -m http.server 8000
npx http-server

Then open http://localhost:8000/dashboard.html or index.html to pick a module.
